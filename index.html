Penny App Support

Welcome to the Penny App Support page. Here you'll find answers to frequently asked questions and resources to help you get the most out of Penny. If you need further assistance, please don't hesitate to contact us.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I log an expense?

You can log an expense by tapping the "Add Expense" button on the home screen. You can either type in your expense details or use the voice input feature to speak your expense. For example, say "Spent $50 on groceries at Whole Foods."

2. Can I edit or delete an expense?

Yes, you can edit or delete an expense. Go to the "Expenses" tab, select the expense you wish to modify, and choose "Edit" or "Delete" from the options.

3. How does the app categorize my expenses?

Penny uses AI-powered algorithms to automatically categorize your expenses based on the description you provide. You can also manually change the category by editing the expense.

4. Is my data secure?

Yes, your data is securely stored using Firebase services by Google. All data is encrypted, and we implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. For more details, please refer to our Privacy Policy.

5. How do I cancel my subscription?

You can manage or cancel your subscription through your Apple ID account settings. Open the App Store, tap on your profile icon, select "Subscriptions," and choose Penny to make changes.

6. How can I export my expense data?

Currently, we are working on adding an export feature. Stay tuned for updates in future releases!

7. Who do I contact for support or feedback?

We'd love to hear from you! Please reach out to us at support@pennyapp.com with any questions, issues, or feedback.
